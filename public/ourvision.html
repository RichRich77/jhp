OUR STORY

JHP was created in memory of Jennifer Hite Parvin. Cancer took Jennifer's life in June of 2011, but it never took her lessons. Jennifer taught by example as she loved everyone around her, and always gave back to her community even through her 6 years of battle with cancer. To keep her charitable character alive JHP was created to provide resources to cancer research. A percentage of every clothing purchased is donated to cancer research and to families dealing with a member fighting cancer.


OUR SYMBOL

Our JHP symbol at first glance looks like a double cancer ribbon, but upon further viewing, you can see that it spells out JHP!
